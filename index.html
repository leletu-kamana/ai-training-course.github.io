Same assignment. Two ways of using AI.

The mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell because it produces ATP through cellular respiration, converting glucose and oxygen into usable energy for the cell. [pasted, unread]

I asked Claude to explain mitochondrial function, then rewrote it in my own words after checking it against my textbook: mitochondria generate the energy cells need by breaking down glucose — this is why muscle cells, which need lots of energy, contain more of them than skin cells. [understood, cited, rewritten]

↑ The difference isn't whether you used AI. It's whether you understood what came out the other end.

What this course won't do

This is not a guide to evading AI-detection software, "humanising" AI text to slip past plagiarism checkers, or hiding AI use from teachers. Those strategies don't make you a better thinker — they just delay the moment you find out you can't do the work yourself. This course is the opposite of that.
